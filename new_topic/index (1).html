About Etherwing Aerospace

Pioneering the future of space technology.

Our Mission

At EtherWing Aerospace, our mission is to push the boundaries of space exploration by developing cutting-edge CubeSat technology. We are dedicated to providing innovative solutions that enable our clients to achieve their goals in the final frontier.

Our History

Founded in 2021, Etherwing Aerospace has grown to become a leading provider of CubeSat technology. With a team of experienced engineers and scientists, we have successfully launched numerous satellites into orbit, contributing to groundbreaking research and discoveries.

Our Products and Services

Innovative solutions for your space exploration needs.

CubeSat Platforms

Customizable CubeSat platforms for your mission requirements.

  • 1U, 2U, and 3U CubeSat platforms
  • Modular design for easy customization
  • Reliable and proven performance

Launch Services

Seamless access to space through our launch partners.

  • Global launch site availability
  • Comprehensive mission management

Ground Support

Reliable ground support services for your space missions.

  • Satellite operations and control
  • Data processing and analysis
  • Telemetry, tracking, and command
